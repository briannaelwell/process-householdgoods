Design Challenge

The following is an excercise from the Human Centered Design Toolkit to help determine a design question for this project.

What is the problen you're trying to solve?

To furnish the homes of those in need in the central and eastern Massachusetts area.

Take a stab at framing it as a design question.

How can the website help furnish the homes of those in need?

Now, state the ultimate impact you're trying to have.

Increase the number of volunteers and goods donations to Household Goods, in turn furnishing more homes.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

  • Simplified copy
  • More clear navigation
  • Imagery
  • Simplify the number of pages by consolidating repeitive content

Finally, write down some of the content and constraints that you're facing.

  • Overwhelming amount of information on the site, may be difficult to organize
  • Making giving donations and receving donations equally prominent

Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

How can the Household Goods website encourage both donations and volunteers, while having clear navigation for those wanting to recieve donations?
