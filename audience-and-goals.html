Audience & Goals

Now that I had determined a design challenge and conducted some research, I could then identify my audiences and goals.

Audience

I began by defining my audience on a broad scale. From there, I then narrowed it down into more specific groups based on their relationship with Household Goods as an organization and a website.

Under Direcetly Involved, I divided my audience into Give Help and Need Help. I also identified some questions that skeptics may have.

The Give Help category consists of people that are involved in the organization in some capacity to assist those in need. I identified the following individuals:

  • Staff
  • Volunteers
  • Board of directors
  • Donators
  • Social service agencies

Need Help encompasses the people that recieve help from Household Goods. This category includes the following:

  • Recently homeless veterans
  • Survivors of domentic violence
  • Victims of disaster
  • Families
  • Low Income
  • Immigrants
  • Those coping with illness or disability
  • Children, youth, or elderly

Finally, I determined my Skeptics. I identified the following possible questions that skeptics may have:

  • Cleanliess of furniture?
  • Quality of furniture?

Goals

With a specific audience in mind, I could now determine practical goals.

Overarching Goals

Client
  • Raise awareness about who they are.
  • Increase number of volunteers.
  • Increase number of furniture donations.
  • Increase monetary donations
  • Partner with more social services.
Audience
  • Learn more about Household Goods.
  • Donate goods and/or money to the organization.
  • Volunteer.
  • Request goods.
Website
  • Make it easy to sign up to volunteer.
  • Make it easy to request goods.

Specific Goals

User Goal Content/Action
Household Goods Staff & Board Increase organization awareness, increase donations. Stories about organization's impact in the community, clear brand identity.
New Volunteers Sign up to volunteer, see specific volunteer events, join a volunteer group. Fill out volunteer form, volunteer time to deliver or sort goods.
Recurring Volunteers Login to schedule a voltunteer time, host a goods drive, head a volunteer group. Volunteer time to deliver or sort goods.
Goods Recievers Request goods, get into contact with Household Goods. Fill out form to request goods, get information for personal situation.
Social Service Agencies Provide a tangible avenue for people to recieve goods & other assistance. Partner with Household Goods.
